My Approach

Holistic Guidance

The IB and GCSE aren’t just about memorising content — they’re about managing time, staying motivated and learning how to think critically. Having graduated with a 41/45 in IB, I share practical strategies for mastering coursework, essays and exam techniques while maintaining balance and wellbeing.

In lessons, we break down complex topics step by step and build a deep understanding of what is needed to succeed. I’ll help you stay organised, balance commitments and develop study habits that last.
