My Approach to Coaching

The IB and GCSE are demanding programmes that require more than rote learning. My coaching emphasises critical thinking, time management and intrinsic motivation so you can truly master your subjects and enjoy the learning journey.

We break down challenging concepts into manageable steps and build from first principles to advanced applications. I guide you through coursework, essays and exam preparation with constructive feedback and proven study strategies.

Balancing academics with wellbeing is vital. I’ll share techniques for organisation, maintaining focus and managing stress so that you remain energised and ready to succeed.
