Study Tips

Advice and resources to enhance your learning

Time Management

Create a realistic study schedule that balances your subjects and extracurricular commitments. Break larger tasks into smaller steps and set clear goals for each study session. Use digital calendars or planners to visualise deadlines and allocate time blocks for revision.

Revision Strategies

Use past papers and practice questions to familiarise yourself with exam formats. Make concise summary notes and flashcards for quick review, and teach the material to someone else to reinforce your understanding. Vary your revision methods by using mind maps, diagrams and practice problems.

Organisation & Wellbeing

Keep your notes and resources organised using folders or digital tools. Ensure you rest and stay active – good sleep, exercise and hobbies help improve concentration and memory. Don’t hesitate to ask for help when you encounter challenging topics; reaching out early prevents stress from building up.
